Run Loop

  1. Mine nearby terrain to stock up on building blocks.
  2. Use movement, timing, and jump pads to clear the obby route.
  3. Meet crew cameos, collect studs, and improvise new paths.
  4. Reach the portal with the whole run completed.

Controls

  • Phone: Use the touch buttons below the game canvas
  • Tap canvas: Aim a block, then mine or place nearby
  • Move: A D or arrow keys
  • Jump: Space or W
  • Sprint: Hold Shift
  • Mine: Left click nearby blocks
  • Place: Right click with a selected block
  • Hotbar: 1 Dirt, 2 Stone, 3 Wood
  • Respawn: R

Mixing the vibes

  • Block terrain, mining, and building create the Minecraft half.
  • Studs, jump pads, checkpoints, and a race-to-portal goal bring the Roblox half.
  • One Piece crew cameos add character stops and hint bubbles throughout the run.
  • Use your collected blocks to bridge gaps or build shortcuts through the obby sections.

Crew Cameos

  • Luffy: Start area momentum

  • Nami: Stud and checkpoint guidance
  • Zoro: Stone-wall shortcut hint
  • Sanji: Final platforming pep talk

Player Mindset

  • Think like a builder when a jump looks too tight.
  • Think like a platformer player when a shortcut looks tempting.
  • Reset often, experiment fast, and keep your momentum up.
